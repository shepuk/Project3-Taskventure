Level Up Your To-Do List

Complete Your Own Quests, Battle Monsters and Collect Rare Artefacts.

Fully Featured Task Manager

Keep track of your day with Taskventure! A helpful task-tracking app designed to keep you organised.

Create and track multiple tasks, mark as complete, delete, edit and more. Sort by date & urgency. Search active tasks with a helpful search query tool.

Your Tasks, Anywhere.

The responsive browser app can be loaded on desktops, laptops, tablets and mobiles. After securely logging in, you can access your quests from anywhere, at any time.

Addictive RPG Elements to Help You Acheive Your Goals.

• Select a hero to represent you, and level up skills as you complete tasks.

• Keep track of your level as you gain experience points. EXP can be gained from completing tasks, defeating enemies and finding treasures.

• Battle monsters as you grow stronger. Each monster has their own requirement so it's important to keep leving up!

• Collect rare treasures as you level up and claim the best rewards.

• Compete with friends to reach the top of the leaderboard.

• Gorgeous fantasy pixel art by OGA and Kyrise.
