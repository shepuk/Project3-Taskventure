Help & About

How to use Taskventure.

Taskventure is a task-tracking app designed to help you organise and track your life. Taskventure also includes fantasy and adventure elements such as heroes, monsters and character stat tracking.


Your character.

When signing up, you can choose a character to represent you. All characters have skills such as strength, intellect and skill. Completing tasks will increase these skills. For instance, you can create a task for doing a workout, and assign the strength stat to it. Upon completion, your strength stat will increase by one.


Enemy Battles.

To fight monsters, you must meet the stat requirement. More fierce enemies, such as dragons, may require up to 20 strength points. And cunning wizards may require a high intellect to defeat.


Treasures.

Collect rare artifacts as you level up. Unlike monsters, finding these treasures requires milestones other than character attributes. You may need to complete a certain number of urgent tasks, or have a particular amount of active current tasks to claim these rewards.


Experience.

Your hero's level increases with experience points. Completing tasks, defeating monsters and collecting treasure will all reward you with these experience points. At 500 EXP, you will gain a level.

About the app.

Taskventure is a project submission for a Web Applications Development course, and is created by Paul Shepherd.


With a focus on backend development, the following technologies are used;

  • Python, PyMongo
  • Flask
  • MongoDB
  • JavaScript, jQuery
  • HTML/CSS
  • Werkzeug

Art was provided by Open Game Art, Kyrise and From Software.
